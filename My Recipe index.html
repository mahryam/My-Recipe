My Favorite Recipe

This is a collection of my favorite recipes

Jollof rice

I prefer to eat to Jollof rice with fried chicken and plaintain popularly known as dodo

List of Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Blended pepper
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Get a pan and pour the vegetable oil to heat it then fry the blended pepper
  2. After frying the pepper, you add seasoning to taste.
  3. Then add water to the fried pepper and boil
  4. Cook to your taste
  5. Fry your plaintain and chicken
  6. Serve your dish
Amala

I prefer to eat amala with ewedu and gbegiri

List of Ingredients
  • Yam flour
  • Water
  • Turning stick
Steps
  1. Boil the water in a pot
  2. Reduce a little out of it then pour the yam glour and turn continuously
  3. Keep turning until desired constitency is reached
  4. Serve with any desired dish

Plantain and Egg

This food is best taken for breakfast

List of Ingredients

  • Plantain
  • Egg
  • Onions, sausage and tomatoes
  • Vegatable oil
  • Frying pan

Steps

  1. Heat the pan for a minute
  2. Then pour the sliced plantain and fry till golden brown
  3. For egg;slice the onions, sausage and tomatoes then fry
  4. Add the egg and mix and fry well
  5. Serve dish with soft drink of your choice

Oatmeal

This meal could be eaten alongside moimoi or akara in the Nigerian Style

Ingredients
  • Oat
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Water
Steps
  1. Mix a little portion of oat in a bowl
  2. Boil enough quantity of water for the oat
  3. Pour the mixed oat in the water and stir gradually and continuously till the desired consistency is met
  4. Serve in a dish, then add sugar and milk to taste

Pap

Just like the Oatmeal, pap could also be taken with moimoi and akara or plantain

Ingredients

  • Cold pap
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Water

Steps

  1. Mix the pap with water in a bowl
  2. Boil the water and add the mixed pap, then stir thoroughly
  3. Keep stirring to prevent lumps
  4. Serve and add sugar with milk to taste
